THE STORY OF HAROLD

How did Harold get here?

Harold was born a poor kid in a poverty-struck land. However, despite the lack of support from his own family, friends, and village, he rose above the negativity, and through the power of his own individuality, Harold created a happy life for himself.

This act of courage attracted the interest of God, who granted Harold superhuman physique and powers. Harold used these powers to help people from around the world. However, upon hearing of this, people around the world seeked to kill Harold in hope to obtain the powers for themselves.

Harold was unable to defend him and his family from these hordes of individuals who eventually captured his family. In order to save his family, Harold offered himself up to the capturers, who hung him.

You found and infiltrated their secret base. They offered you to play a game of hangman against you with Harold's life on the line. You are the only one who can save Harold! With just one word, you can save his life.
